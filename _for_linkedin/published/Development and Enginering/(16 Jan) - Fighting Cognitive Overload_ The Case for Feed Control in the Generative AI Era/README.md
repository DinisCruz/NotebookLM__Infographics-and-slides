## Key Themes

- **Cognitive Load**: Every piece of content in our feeds carries a cognitive cost
- **Platform Limitations**: LinkedIn allows blocking users but not company pages
- **AI-Generated Spam**: Generative AI has dramatically worsened signal-to-noise ratio
- **Quality over Quantity**: Rising appreciation for authentic, human-generated content
- **User Empowerment**: The need for better filtering tools and user control
